French Toast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Directions

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt in a shallow bowl.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Dip bread in egg mixture, coating both sides.
  4. Cook bread until golden brown, 2-3 minutes per side.
  5. Serve with maple syrup and enjoy!
