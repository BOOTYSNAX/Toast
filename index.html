French Toast Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 4 slices bread
  • Butter (for frying)
  • Syrup, powdered sugar, or fruit (optional)

Directions:

  1. Whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon in a bowl.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the mixture, coating both sides.
  3. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Cook bread slices until golden brown on each side (about 2â€“3 minutes).
  5. Serve warm with syrup, powdered sugar, or fruit.
